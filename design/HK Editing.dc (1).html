About

The reader your book deserves before anyone else meets it

I'm Hannah — an editor who has spent the better part of a decade in the quiet company of other people's sentences. I came to this work the long way, through bookshops and slush piles and far too many late nights with a red pencil, and I've never quite gotten over the thrill of helping a story find its truest shape.

Every manuscript that lands on my desk gets the same thing: unhurried attention, an honest ear, and a deep respect for the voice that's already there. I'm not here to make your book sound like mine — I'm here to make it sound unmistakably, confidently like you.

— Hannah K.
